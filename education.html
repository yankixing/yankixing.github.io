Media and Education



Medaia like Newspapers, Journals and Magazines are the basic oldest channels of communication between one source to another. Print media are being printed in every regional language to facilitate the readers to get information and knowledge in ones own mother tongue.

Book is the best source of education. A lot of material of formal education is available in the books. Mostly students depend upon this medium to learn the education. In spite of modern technology, still this medium is popular for the learning.

Internet is a medium, which made the world a Global Village. Around the world students are being globally connected with one another via Internet. It has been seen that large number of people depend on the Internet to collect information, read news, play games and also for searching process and business work.
